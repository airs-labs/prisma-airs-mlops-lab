# HuggingFace Integration

## Topics to Cover (in order)
1. PANW-HuggingFace partnership -- what it means, why it exists
2. Public scan results on HuggingFace -- where to find them, what they show
3. Free vs paid AIRS -- public HF scans vs enterprise scanning with custom policies
4. HF-specific evaluations -- verified organizations, license compliance checks
5. Source type HUGGING_FACE -- scanning HF models via AIRS with HF source type

## How to Explore
- Visit HuggingFace model pages and look for PANW security scan badges/results
- Compare: what does a public HF scan show vs what the full AIRS SDK returns?
- This project's HF security group: `00000000-0000-0000-0000-000000000003`

## Student Activities
- Find PANW scan results on a popular HuggingFace model page
- Compare the public scan output to what you get from the AIRS SDK
- Discuss: what would a customer get from AIRS that they cannot get from the free HF scans?

## Customer Talking Point
"Customers using HuggingFace models already benefit from the PANW partnership for basic scanning. AIRS adds custom security policies, pipeline integration, blocking enforcement, and scanning of private/internal models that never touch HuggingFace."
